Features of Meta Ray‑Ban Smart Glasses

Camera & Media

The Meta Ray‑Ban smart glasses are equipped with a 12 MP ultra-wide camera, capable of capturing sharp photos and immersive POV videos in high resolution. You can record videos up to 1080p or 3K depending on the model. The glasses also support live streaming directly to Instagram and Facebook, and provide dedicated capture controls for photos and video via touch or voice commands. A privacy LED indicator ensures transparency by showing when recording is active.

Audio & Communication

Enjoy directional open-ear speakers that deliver private sound while keeping you aware of your surroundings. The five-microphone array ensures crystal-clear voice capture for calls or recordings, even in noisy environments. Hands-free calling and messaging are supported through Bluetooth, and integration with Shazam and live translation allows you to identify music or translate speech in real time. AI voice assistant controls enable effortless operation of music, recording, calls, and other smart features.

Connectivity & Compatibility

With Bluetooth 5.3 and Wi-Fi 6E, these glasses maintain fast and stable connections with your phone or companion app. The Meta View app allows you to sync, manage content, and control settings on iOS and Android devices. The glasses integrate seamlessly with popular social apps like Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger.

Battery & Usage

The smart glasses provide around 6–8 hours of typical usage on a single charge. A portable charging case is included to provide multiple additional charges on the go.

Design & Comfort

Designed for everyday use, the frames are lightweight, comfortable, and water-resistant (IPX4). Many models are compatible with prescription lenses, and you can choose from over 150 frame and lens combinations to personalize your style.

Advanced & AR Models

For higher-end models with AR displays, you can access navigation, real-time captions, object recognition, and contextual AI assistance. Gesture and neural band controls allow advanced interaction via wristband or muscle signals. Real-time contextual AI helps with object identification, menu translations, and environmental awareness while wearing the glasses.

Touch & Interaction

Swipe or tap along the frame to adjust volume, control media, or navigate through various features quickly and intuitively.
